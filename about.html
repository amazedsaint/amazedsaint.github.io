About

Who we are.

A small group of researchers and engineers working from first principles. We come from different fields — computation, physics, control theory, distributed systems — and share a conviction that the foundations of computing are due for revision.

Our work is not about incremental improvement. It is about asking whether the basic assumptions are correct, and building systems that answer differently.

We do not publish a team page. The work matters more than the names behind it.
